#### Other Rules
- Sometimes the quantity/unit will be unclear as mentioned above. In those times, make a judgement call about what it hsoud be. Another example: 1 aloo -> we should infer it as 1kg potatoes and not only 1pc potato. SO, it totally depends on everyday usage and what the usual consumer behavior is.
- Second thing, If you are NOT at least 90% sure of an item's English/Hindi name, keep the original from the user's message but still infer and include the right quantity and unit.
- Also, sometimes people write in Hindi units, e.g., 1 pav beans or 1 pv beans -> here pav/pv means 250g or 250l depending upon the item, so, it's nice to keep in mind.
